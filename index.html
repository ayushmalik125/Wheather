About Us

The Weather Network delivers critical, timely forecasts and alerts to help you plan your day and stay safe, wherever you are.
We use advanced technology and a dedicated team to ensure you're ready for every weather moment, big or small.

Our Vision

We are a small team of meteorology enthusiasts and data experts committed to one goal: making sure the weather never catches you by surprise. Your safety, comfort, and ability to plan are our top priority.

Our Partners

We collaborate with national meteorological agencies, including the MetOffice and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA). We are honoured to be members of international organisations and agencies, including the World Meteorological Organisation (WMO) and the European Space Agency (ESA), as well as industry societies and associations like RMetS and HMEl.
